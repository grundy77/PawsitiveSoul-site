Privacy Policy – PawsitiveSoul

Last updated: August 26, 2025

PawsitiveSoul provides a daily motivational notification with an animal image and a quote.

Information We Collect

  • We do not collect, store, or sell any personal data.
  • Your chosen animal and notification time are stored only on your device.

How We Deliver Content

  • Images and quotes are served from our backend (Supabase) when a notification is shown.
  • No personally identifiable information is sent to our servers.

Analytics & Tracking

  • No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs are included.
  • If a future “like/love” button is enabled, taps may be logged anonymously to improve content quality.

Notifications

The app schedules daily local notifications at the time you choose. You may change or revoke this permission anytime in your device settings.

Children’s Privacy

PawsitiveSoul is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.
